Baking a simple but delicious Carob Cake

Recipe
  • 225 g butter
  • 2c flour
  • 2t baking powder
  • 2 c coconut
  • 1.5 c milk
  • 1 c raw sugar
  • 2T carob powder
  • 2t vanilla

    • Melt the butter and mix into the dry ingredients. Add sultanas vanilla and milk and stir well.
    Bake at 180 degrees Celsius for 30 minutes in a shallow lined dish.
    Ice with carob icing.
