What we do

We specialise in the design, development, implementation and support of customised database applications. Our clients are largely not-for-profit and government sector organisations and we have worked extensively with Iwi and other Maori Social Service Providers. Data People Ltd has extensive experience with the Microsoft Office suite of applications and we have used Microsoft Access as our primary database development tool. More recently we have moved towards the development of browser based applications written using the Visual Studio suite in combination with SQL server.

Many of our customers need database applications to store and report information about their client base and the work done with these clients. We have many customers who deliver social support services to their clients and need to report on a quarterly and annual basis to their Funders. We have found Microsoft Access to be a powerful but inexpensive development tool which allows for fast and flexible database development and low implementation costs.
